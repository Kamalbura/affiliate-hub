How to Choose the Right Ceiling Fan

Size Matters

For rooms up to 75 sq.ft, choose fans with blade span of 36" or less. For 75-175 sq.ft rooms, 42-48" fans work best. For larger rooms, go for 52" or more.

Energy Efficiency

Look for BEE star ratings - higher stars mean more efficiency. BLDC motors consume up to 60% less electricity than regular fans.

Air Delivery

Check for high air delivery (measured in CMM). Good ceiling fans should deliver at least 210-220 CMM for effective cooling.
