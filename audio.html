How to Choose the Right Audio Device

Headphone Types

Over-ear headphones offer best sound quality but are bulkier. On-ear are more portable. In-ear earbuds are most compact but may sacrifice sound quality.

Sound Quality

Look for balanced sound with good bass response, clear mids, and crisp highs. Consider frequency response range (20Hz-20kHz is standard).

Battery Life

For wireless devices, consider battery life. Good earbuds offer 5-8 hours, while premium headphones can offer 20-30+ hours with the charging case.
