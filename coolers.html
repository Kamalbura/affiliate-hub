How to Choose the Right Air Cooler

Room Size Matters

For small rooms (up to 150 sq. ft), choose coolers with 15-20L capacity. For medium rooms (150-250 sq. ft), go for 20-40L. For larger spaces, look for 40-60L capacity.

Air Delivery

Check the air delivery rate (measured in cubic meters per hour or CMH). Higher CMH means better cooling. Look for at least 1500-2000 CMH for effective cooling.

Water Tank Capacity

A larger tank means less frequent refilling. For continuous use, look for 25+ liters capacity. Some models offer auto-fill feature for added convenience.
