How to Choose the Right Camera

Camera Type

DSLR cameras offer versatility and control. Mirrorless cameras are compact with similar quality. Point-and-shoot cameras are best for beginners and casual use.

Sensor Size

Larger sensors (full-frame) capture more light and detail. APS-C sensors balance performance and portability. Micro Four Thirds are compact with good quality.

Megapixels

Higher megapixels allow for larger prints and cropping. For most users, 20-24MP is sufficient. For professional use, consider 30MP+ cameras.
